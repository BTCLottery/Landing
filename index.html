Interoperability

The Lottery Chain bridges multiple blockchain networks

What is the Lottery Chain ?


The Lottery Chain started as a Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum that proved to be the perfect fit to bridge multiple blockchains. It is a network of DPoS nodes that allow highly-scalable Games and DApps while still being backed by the security of the blockchain that is linked to.


Interoperability is one of the core features that will take blockchains to the next level, by allowing the migration of assets like Coins, Tokens, NFTs between multiple chains. Because of current Blockchain's limitations regarding speed and costs, the Lottery Chain offers a viable and easy way to migrate assets between different networks, due to its 1 second block time with 10.000 transactions per second and its fee-less environment.
